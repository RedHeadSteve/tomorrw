Our services

Startup pack - A Great Start for …

Do you want to give your business the best start possible? The Startup Pack of Tomorrw Consultancy is developed to facilitate startup entrepreneurs in building a strong foundation for their business. This package includes step-by-step guidance and hands on support.

Depending on your specific demands our team guides you in topics such as conducting market research, developing a validated business plan, and preparing all funding documentation. Our team also has specialists available to join or even lead negotiations for our clients with their (potential investors), whether it’s through a loan, convertible or shares.

The startup pack is built to achieve maximal result for minimal costs, and is therefore always customized to each clients specific needs.

Scaleup program

With growing businesses come new challenges. The main specialization of Tomorrw Consultancy is to professionalize (early) scaling businesses. Guiding multiple companies through these processes over the years, our program is built around the seven key indicators most crucial to the failure or success of every business.

These key indicators were used building this program in three categories, which all entail several modules.

  • - Team, Culture & Leadership
  • - Organizational Structure, Business Processes & Effective Operations
  • - Scalability and Sustainable Growth

Through the Scale-up program Tomorrw Consultancy enables you to create the best possible conditions for long term success.

Social Workfloor Safety

Bullying, prejudice, a toxic work culture. A majority of professionals will have at least one job where they have bad experiences like these. But also, when a work culture is relatively good, there’s still a lot to win through our Social Workfloor Safety program. Developed in an exclusive partnership with the Respect Foundation and learning from the most recent studies, this program proofs itself by her results. Increased productivity, less absences, loyal employees, more innovative and creative solutions, and a strong employer brand are just a grasp of the results we see.

Through training, advice, and an awareness program our team is there, for you.
